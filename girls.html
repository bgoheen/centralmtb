Girls Try-It-Ride

Getting more girls on bikes — because everyone deserves to experience the trail.

Come Ride With Us

Our Girls Try-It-Ride is a free, low-pressure opportunity for female students in grades 6–12 to experience mountain biking with our team. You do not need any experience — our coaches will guide you through the basics in a fun, supportive environment.

This is a Try-It ride, not a tryout. Everyone is welcome, and everyone makes the team.

What to Bring

  • A mountain bike (if you have one)
  • A helmet (required)
  • Water bottle
  • Closed-toe shoes

No bike? No problem. We may have loaner bikes available. Contact us ahead of time so we can try to arrange one for you.

When & Where

Girls Try-It-Ride dates are announced before each season. Check back here or sign up for updates to be notified when the next ride is scheduled.

Parents are welcome to stay and attend a short information session with our coaches during the ride.
