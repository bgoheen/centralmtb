Girls Program

Getting more girls on bikes — because everyone deserves to experience the trail.

MCA GRRLS

MCA GRRLS is the Minnesota Cycling Association's initiative dedicated to attracting and retaining female student-athletes, coaches, and volunteers in the mountain biking community.

While MCA programs provide equal opportunities, MCA GRRLS recognizes that females may need different approaches at times to feel genuinely welcome. The program offers girls-only events, rides, camps, and socializing opportunities.

What We Offer

  • Girls-only rides and skill sessions
  • A welcoming, supportive community of female riders
  • Connections to MCA GRRLS events across Minnesota
  • Experienced coaches who understand the importance of inclusivity

Interested in joining or learning more? Email us or sign up and let us know your rider is interested in girls-specific programming.
