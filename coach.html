Become a Coach

Our team is volunteer-run — and we are always looking for help.

Why Coach?

Coaching with Central MTB is a rewarding way to share your love of mountain biking with the next generation. You do not need to be an expert rider — you just need enthusiasm, patience, and a willingness to learn.

Requirements

All coaches are licensed through the Minnesota Cycling Association (MCA). There are three license levels with increasing requirements. Here are the basics to get started:

  • Complete a background check (Level 1)
  • Complete online training — concussion, abuse awareness, and Youth Development Through Cycling (Level 1)
  • First aid and CPR certification (Level 2)
  • Attend MCA Spring Summit for new coaches (Level 3)
  • Complete on-bike skills training and field work hours (Level 2 and 3)

MCA provides comprehensive coach education including concussion training, athlete safety, risk management, and on-the-bike skills development. We will walk you through the entire process.

Interested? Email us and we will walk you through the process. Parents who want to participate in one ride can do so after signing a waiver.
