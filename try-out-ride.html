Try-Out-Ride

Come ride with us — no experience required, no commitment, just fun on the trails.

What is a Try-Out-Ride?

A Try-Out-Ride is a free, low-pressure opportunity for students in grades 6–12 to experience mountain biking with our team. You do not need any experience — our coaches will guide you through the basics in a fun, safe environment.

This is a Try-It ride, not a Try-Out ride. Everyone is welcome, and everyone makes the team.

What to Bring

  • A mountain bike (if you have one)
  • A helmet (required)
  • Water bottle
  • Closed-toe shoes

No bike? No problem. We may have loaner bikes available. Contact us ahead of time so we can try to arrange one for you.

When & Where

Try-Out-Ride dates are announced before each season. Check back here or sign up for updates to be notified when the next ride is scheduled.

Parents are welcome to stay and attend a short information session with our coaches during the ride.
