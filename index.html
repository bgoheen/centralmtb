About Us

A Supportive Community
on Two Wheels

We're a youth mountain biking team where riders of all abilities explore the Twin Cities' best trails — Battle Creek, Lebanon Hills, Theo Wirth, and more. Our July through October season includes coaching, practice rides, and optional MCA racing.

New to mountain biking? Perfect. We'll teach you everything, help you earn a varsity letter, and make sure you have a blast doing it.

Practice Schedule

Tuesday & Thursday evenings, 5:30–7:30pm. Saturday mornings, 9:30–11:30am. We ride trails like Battle Creek, Carver Lake, and Sunfish Park.

🏔️

All Levels Welcome

No experience needed. Our coaches work with every skill level from first-time riders to experienced racers. Racing is completely optional.

🏫

Who Can Join

Students grades 6–12 from St. Paul Central, Harding, Johnson, Como Park, and most other St. Paul schools. Not sure? Ask us.

FAQs

Common Questions

No prior experience is needed! Our team welcomes riders of all skill levels, from beginners to experienced cyclists.
Riders need a mountain bike (hardtail or full suspension), helmet, and basic gear like gloves and water bottles. See our equipment guide for details, and contact us if you need help finding affordable options.
Practices are Tuesday and Thursday evenings and Saturday mornings at Twin Cities trails. The season runs the first week of July through mid-October. Participation is flexible based on your schedule.
Racing is completely optional! Many riders focus on skill-building and trail rides while others compete in Minnesota Cycling Association (MCA) races. You decide what's right for you.
We welcome students in grades 6–12 from St. Paul Central, Harding, Johnson, Como Park, and most other St. Paul schools. Highland Park students should contact the SPY MTB team. Not sure if you're eligible? Contact us and we'll help!
Team registration is $90 per student plus a $100 MCA fee. Racing is optional — if you choose to compete, race fees are separate. See the cost breakdown above for the full picture.
