Meet Our Coaches

The volunteer coaches who make Central MTB possible.

Our coaching staff is made up of dedicated volunteers who are passionate about mountain biking and helping young riders develop their skills, confidence, and love for the sport. All coaches are licensed through NICA and the Minnesota Cycling Association.

Want to Coach?

We are always looking for volunteers. Whether you are an experienced rider or just getting started, there is a place for you on our coaching team.
