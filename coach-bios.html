Meet Our Coaches

The volunteer coaches who make Central MTB possible.

Our coaching staff is made up of dedicated volunteers who are passionate about mountain biking and helping young riders develop their skills, confidence, and love for the sport. All coaches are licensed through the Minnesota Cycling Association.
